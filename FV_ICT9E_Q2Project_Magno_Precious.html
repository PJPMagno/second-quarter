Philippines is an archipelago as it consists of thousands of islands. It is a country full of stunning tourists spots, natural resources and accomodating people. Philippines is the go-to country of internationals because of its beautiful scenery.

Siargao is known as the "Surfing Capital of the Philippines" It has the most perfect scenery and it is the perfect place to visit when you love surfing!

Palawan is kwown to have the clereast water and stunning limestone cliffs, it has a white sand beach.
